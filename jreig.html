Johnson Real Estate Investment Group, Ltd.

Ruby on Rails | Logo Redesign | Digital Design

The challenge

Our client's ten year old business was experiencing a season of rapid expansion. Jason needed automation of his manual processes, previously implemented through Excel macros. The growth of his business necessitated a system that could be used and managed by his whole team. And finally, his customer facing site was built on a basic template and needed a facelift.


The solution

To save time and reduce human error, we created two applications:


The first; a property comparison tool that allows Jason and his team to quickly compare 500,000+ properties, controlling for custom factors.

And second, a cost estimator tool that automates the home evaluation process based on a configurable database of itemized costs.


To modernize his now 10 year old business, we also updated his logo and public facing website to reflect the success of his burgeoning company.
