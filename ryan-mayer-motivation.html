Squarespace | Logo Design | Digital Design

The challenge

When the client reached out for assistance, he had already started a Squarespace site. He quickly realized he did not have the time to finish it himself. He needed a site that promoted his personal brand, highlighted his experience and demonstrated his value.


The solution

The site design is centered around the professional photos Ryan had commissioned. The result is a place for visitors to not only find the services they need, but also get a feel for Ryan himself. The photographs convey exactly how Ryan is in real life; optimistic, full of energy and ready to help others. Also reinforced is his experience with a blog space, testimonials and video.
