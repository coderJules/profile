McCreary's Healthy Homes

Square Commerce Website | Custom graphic design | Online booking setup

The work

As an established carpet cleaning expanding into the car detailing market, the owner needed a new site to allow customers to understand their menu items, as well have the ability to book online.


Additionally, their industry software lacked the functionality to send personalized mailers to clients, resulting in missed opportunities for repeat business from loyal customers.

The solution

To overcome these challenges, custom graphics were created to seamlessly align the site with their established branding. Their extensive menu was reimagined into an intuitive and user-friendly online booking system, powered by Acuity Scheduling software.


To address the software limitations, a custom integration was developed to enable communication between two third-party platforms. This solution empowers Kevin to strategically target the right customers with marketing postcards, maximizing engagement and fostering client loyalty.
